The Florida Panthers are a professional hockey team originating from Sunrise, FL. They are members of the Atlantic Division of the Eastern Conference of the National Hockey League (NHL). Their inaugural season was in 1993-94 and the team made the Stanley Cup Finals by 1996, becoming (up until 2018) the fastest team to ever reach the Finals from their inaugural season.
